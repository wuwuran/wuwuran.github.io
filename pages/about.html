Profile

  • Innovative Urban Designer, Researcher, and emerging academic with a robust blend of industry experience and scholarly expertise.
  • PhD research specialises in an AI-driven taxonomy for urban screens and augmented public spaces, employing data-driven (machine learning) clustering methodologies and creating an Augmented Public Spaces Index (APSI) to assess the quality of spaces.
  • Interdisciplinary approach combining urban design, digital technologies, and social sciences to address urban challenges.
  • An urban designer with five years of experience in China's highly competitive construction industry.
  • International experience through education in the UK and professional experience in China, offering diverse perspectives on urban planning.
  • Bilingual in Mandarin (native) and English.
  • Continuing involvement in voluntary activities; keen independent traveller and photographer.
